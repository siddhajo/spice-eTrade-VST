Two Tally companies for ledgers: Sales Party Ledgers import buyer-side parties into the Tally company. RD & URD Purchase Party Ledgers import into the Tally company (since suppliers are ASP's). All vouchers currently import into ISP — open the matching company in Tally before importing.

▼ 🗺️ E-way Bill Distance Per-invoice override

ISP sales vouchers carry a <DISTANCE> field used by the e-way bill portal. Click NIC to look up the distance on the official portal (captcha-gated; one route at a time), paste the result, click Save. The value is saved per route, so every other invoice between the same two PINs — this auction and all future ones — picks it up automatically. The auto badge marks rows that resolved via the saved route table.
Pick an auction and click Refresh.
How to import into Tally:
  1. Open your Tally company (must match the name in Settings → To Tally).
  2. Press Alt+O → Import → Vouchers.
  3. Select the downloaded .xml file and confirm.
  4. Tally rejects the import if any ledger name in the XML doesn't exist in your company — make sure the ledgers in Settings → To Tally match your Tally setup exactly.
